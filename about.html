About the Hosts

Dawa & Pemba
Tamang

Saipatri Homestay began as our family home on a quiet slope in Kalimpong, where mornings arrive with drifting clouds and the smell of wet pine. We grew up in these hills and learned early that the mountains move at their own pace — generous and patient.

Our days are rooted in local rhythms, from tending the kitchen garden to sharing tea with neighbors after market trips. The stories, songs, and recipes we pass to our children come from this landscape, and hosting allows us to keep those traditions alive in a living, everyday way.

We opened our doors so travelers could experience Kalimpong as a home, not just a stop. When guests stay with us, we hope they leave with warm food, restful sleep, and a quiet memory of mountain light that lingers long after the journey back.
