About the Hosts

Babin & Geeta
Your Mountain Family

Saipatri Homestay began as our family home on a quiet hillside in Echhey, Kalimpong, where mornings arrive with drifting mist and the scent of wet pine. We grew up in these hills and learned early that the mountains move at their own pace — generous and patient.

Our days are rooted in local rhythms, from tending the kitchen garden to preparing three fresh meals a day with locally available ingredients. The stories, songs, and recipes we share with our guests come from this very landscape, and hosting allows us to keep those traditions alive in a living, everyday way.

We opened our doors so travelers could experience Echhey and Kalimpong as a home, not just a stop. Every stay includes breakfast, lunch, and dinner — all freshly made. Check in and check out as it suits you; there are no strict timings here, only warmth. When guests stay with us, we hope they leave with nourishing food, restful sleep, and a quiet memory of mountain light that lingers long after the journey back.
